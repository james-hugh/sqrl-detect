## 2026-02-19 - Enhancing Visual Hierarchy and Accessibility
**Learning:** Using ANSI color codes improves the visual hierarchy of the CLI, but they must be paired with textual labels (e.g., [SECURE], [CRITICAL]) to ensure accessibility for color-blind users or non-color terminals.
**Action:** Implemented a tiered, color-coded threat meter that includes both descriptive labels and color indicators to represent different alert states.
